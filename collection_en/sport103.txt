<b>Olympics:</b> Samaranch dies

Samaranch: IOC president for 21 years

Former International Olympic Committee president Juan Antonio Samaranch has died at the age of 89.

Samaranch held his position for 21 years until 2001 and is credited as the man who brought professional sport into the Olympics.

IOC president Jacques Rogge, who succeeded Samaranch, said: "I cannot find the words to express the distress of the Olympic Family."
