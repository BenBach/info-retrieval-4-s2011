Championships returning to Switzerland

Last updated: 1st May 2010   Subscribe to RSS Feed
<b>Athletics:</b> Zurich to host Euros

The European Championships in 2014 will be held in Zurich, the European Athletics Council has announced.

Zurich will be the second Swiss city to hold the event after Bern hosted the championships in 1954.

European Athletics president Hansjorg Wirz said: "We are very pleased to confirm that Zurich will be the hosts of the European Athletics Championships in 2014.

"Athletics is a hugely popular sport in Switzerland and we have been assured today that we can look forward to a fantastic event."

This year's championships will take place in Barcelona from July 26 to August 1.
